\section*{The Project}

\begin{tcolorbox}[colback=purple!5, colframe=purple, arc=8pt, boxrule=2pt]
    \large
    \textbf{Zara: Light \& Shadow} is an interactive visual novel that tells the story of an aspiring model navigating her first professional photography session. Through player choices, the story explores themes of \textbf{artistic expression, personal boundaries, vulnerability, and creative collaboration}.
\end{tcolorbox}

\vspace{0.5cm}

\subsection*{What Makes This Special?}

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*, itemsep=12pt]
    \item \textbf{Character-Driven Narrative:} Zara's journey from uncertainty to confident self-expression
    \item \textbf{Multiple Story Paths:} Player choices lead to different outcomes and endings
    \item \textbf{Authentic Representation:} Based on real photography sessions and genuine artistic collaboration
    \item \textbf{Diverse Visual Themes:} From editorial fashion to intimate portraiture
    \item \textbf{Respectful Approach:} Consent-based storytelling with clear boundaries
\end{itemize}

\subsection*{The Story Setting}

\begin{tcolorbox}[scenecard]
    \textbf{Time:} A single day in a professional photography studio\\
    \textbf{Duration:} 45-60 minute interactive experience\\
    \textbf{Genre:} Drama, Romance, Slice-of-Life\\
    \textbf{Rating:} Mature (18+) - Artistic nudity with consent mechanics
\end{tcolorbox}

\section*{Frequently Asked Questions}

\subsection*{Compensation \& Contracts}

\textbf{Q: How much does this photoshoot pay?}\\
A: We offer competitive day rates comparable to editorial and commercial modeling work, plus additional usage rights compensation. Specific rates will be discussed during the interview process based on experience and negotiation.

\vspace{0.3cm}

\textbf{Q: Will there be a contract?}\\
A: Yes, a comprehensive contract detailing compensation, usage rights, shoot schedule, and all terms will be provided before any work begins.

\vspace{0.3cm}

\textbf{Q: Can I use the photos for my portfolio?}\\
A: Absolutely! You retain full rights to use all images for your modeling portfolio, social media, and promotional purposes.

\subsection*{Content \& Comfort}

\textbf{Q: Am I required to do intimate/nude photography?}\\
A: No. Intimate or artistic nude shoots are completely optional and require explicit written consent. The project has multiple endings and paths that don't require this content.

\vspace{0.3cm}

\textbf{Q: What if I'm uncomfortable with a particular shot or concept?}\\
A: Your comfort and consent are paramount. You have full authority to decline any shot, concept, or styling choice at any time. We'll work collaboratively to find alternatives.

\vspace{0.3cm}

\textbf{Q: Can I bring a chaperone?}\\
A: Yes, you're welcome to bring a trusted person to all shoots. We encourage models to have support present for comfort and safety.

\subsection*{Logistics}

\textbf{Q: Where are the shoots located?}\\

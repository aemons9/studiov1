How to Export Posts for Instagram

Each post is designed at 1080x1080px (Instagram square format). To export:

  • Browser Screenshot: Click "View Full Size", then use your browser's screenshot tool or a screenshot extension to capture at full resolution.
  • Developer Tools: Open DevTools (F12), go to Device Mode, set dimensions to 1080x1080, and capture screenshot.
  • HTML to Image: Use tools like html2canvas, Puppeteer, or online HTML-to-image converters.
  • Print to PDF: Print the page to PDF and convert to image format.

# Project 08: Urban Collection

## Project Title
```
Urban Collection — Street Meets Couture
```

## Project Subtitle
```
Contemporary Urban Fashion | AI-Generated Street Style
```

## Project Description
```
Where the city becomes the runway and concrete becomes the canvas. This collection explores the intersection of street style and high fashion, generated through AI to capture the energy of urban fashion culture.

The Urban Narrative

URBAN POETRY
The city is our runway. The streets are our stage. Fashion finds its voice in urban landscapes.

URBAN EDIT — 4-Photo Grid
The complete urban narrative in four frames. A comprehensive look at street-level style.

STREET COUTURE — Contemporary Feature
Where haute meets street. Where elegance meets edge. The fusion that defines modern fashion.

STREET LUXE — Photo Stack
Luxury doesn't need a penthouse. Sometimes it walks the streets. High-end aesthetics in everyday contexts.

Visual Language
• Raw urban textures contrasted with refined fashion
• Natural city light and shadow play
• Authentic street environments
• Movement and energy in composition
• Mix of posed and candid aesthetics

The urban collection demonstrates AI's ability to capture not just fashion, but the cultural context in which fashion lives and breathes.
```

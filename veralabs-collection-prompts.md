## Technical Photography Guidelines

### Camera Settings:
- **Aperture**: f/1.4 - f/2.8 for intimate portraits, f/4 - f/5.6 for full body
- **ISO**: 100-400 for maximum detail and minimal noise
- **Shutter**: 1/125 or faster to capture sharp details

### Lighting Principles:
- **Golden Hour**: Side-lighting for dimensional shadows
- **Blue Hour**: Mixed lighting for atmospheric depth
- **Window Light**: North-facing for soft, even illumination
- **Practical Lights**: Candles, lamps for ambient warmth

### Composition:
- **Rule of Thirds**: Model positioned at intersection points
- **Negative Space**: 40-60% empty space for luxury feel
- **Leading Lines**: Architecture guiding eye to subject
- **Frame Within Frame**: Architectural elements as natural frames

### Post-Processing:
- **Color Grading**: Subtle, naturalistic tones
- **Skin Retouching**: Minimal, maintaining texture
- **Fabric Detail**: Enhanced texture visibility
- **Atmospheric Effects**: Subtle haze or light leaks

## ‚ú® Post-Processing Recommendations

1. **Sprites**: Use Remove.bg or rembg for perfect transparency
2. **Backgrounds**: Slight blur in Photoshop (Gaussian Blur 1-2px) for cinematic softness
3. **Color Grading**: Apply complementary color theory (teal/orange)
4. **Sharpening**: Smart sharpen at 0.5-1.0 radius for crisp details
5. **Format**: Sprites as PNG-24 with alpha, Backgrounds as high-quality JPG (95% quality)
